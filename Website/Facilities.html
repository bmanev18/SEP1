Facilities & Services


For over four centuries,Overlook hotel has striven towards becoming the premier
in delivering the very best in facilities to accomodate and exemplify
each of our guests experiences

Golf Course

A state of the art golf course which boasts 25 holes along with a
training facility for beginners and guests without a club membership required

There is a clubhouse with a small bar which serve alcoholic beverages
as well as light refreshments

Proper outfits are mandatory and golf clubs and carts will be provided



Restaurant & Bar

Enjoy dishes from diffrent countries such as indian,french,italian etc in an authentic setting with
great ambience and music from our talented jazz band The Ink Spots

You can enjoy lunch in our well-stocked restaurant from 12:00 to 16:00 everyday and
dinner from 19:00 to 23:00



Spa

The hotel features a sauna and a spa free of charge for hotel guests
where one can unwind and relax and simply enjoy the benefits

Contact the front desk if you would like to book a special treatment



Excursions

The hotel offers a variety of expeditions and activites
for guests of all ages with the proper safety and precaustions guaranteed
Activities:

Hiking

Ski-ing

Horse riding

Canoeing



Fitness Centre

The hotel features a fitness room equipped
with cardio-training and body-building machines located in the Wellness Centre.

Guests will be able to consult with our on-site
health professional who can assist you if the need arises

The gym is open from 8:00 to 19:00



Theatre

The theatre features a variety of plays weekly with famous persons
and can also be booked to play certain films or any other movies
at the request of guests who book the room in advance in the reception

The schedule is posted infront of the hotel and is open to all guests



Conference Rooms

In auditorium, schoolroom or cabaret set-up, or as syndicate meeting rooms,
we can adapt our spaces to meet your requirements.

All meeting rooms are spacious and have projectors and can be catered by the restaurant
present in the hotel

We can accomodate from between 6 to 40 people
